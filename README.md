<h1 align="center"> BPM-to-ms (console app) </h1>
<p> My first conception of BPM to ms conversion, in a console. </p>
<h1>The Math</h1>

> ((60000/BPM) * Note) * Variation

<p>
  **Notes:**
  
  **Whole note:** 4x <br />
  **Half note:** 2x <br />
  **Quarter note:** 1x <br />
  **Eighth note:** 0.5x <br />
  **16th note:** 0.25x <br />
  **32nd note:** 0.125x <br />
  <br />
  **Variations:** <br />
  **Normal:** 1x <br />
  **Dotted:** 1.5x <br />
  **Triplet:** 0.666x <br />
</p>
